DV360 Bid Optimizer

Adjusts live CPM bids based on CVR performance, delivery pacing, and campaign end date. Live bids and daily impression budgets are always fetched from the DV360 API.

Decision Logic
Days Remaining Performance Delivery (yesterday / daily budget) Action Rationale
≤ 3 days Any Any — Hold Protect delivery near end of flight
> 3 days High Performer < 80% ↑ Bid Up CVR above goal, room to buy more delivery
> 3 days High Performer 80–95% — Hold Pacing well, no intervention needed
> 3 days High Performer > 95% ↓ Bid Down Capping — higher bid buys same volume at higher CPM
> 3 days Low Performer > 95% ↓ Bid Down CVR below goal and capping — reduce CPM waste
> 3 days Low Performer ≤ 95% — Hold Not capping — bid reduction risks further under-delivery

Fetching Live Data

Each line item fetches two fields from DV360: the current bid (from bidStrategy) and the daily impression budget (from budget.maxAmount). Delivery ratio is then calculated client-side against impressions_yesterday. Up to 10 concurrent requests.
